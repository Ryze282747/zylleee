Cigarettes After Sex is an American dream pop band formed in El Paso, Texas, in 2008 by Greg Gonzalez, their consistent songwriter and vocalist. Known for their ethereal, dream-like sound and romantic, introspective lyrics, the band gained global popularity through music recommendations, notably the sleeper hit "Nothing's Gonna Hurt You Baby" in 2016. After releasing their debut EP, I., in 2012, their debut album Cigarettes After Sex followed in 2017, with subsequent albums Cry (2019) and X's(2024).

Cigarettes After Sex's most notable works include their self-titled debut album (Cigarettes After Sex, 2017), their second album (Cry, 2019), and their third album (X's, 2024). Key singles that launched their career and remain popular are the sleeper hit "Nothing's Gonna Hurt You Baby" from their 2012 EP, I, and the popular song "Apocalypse" from their debut album, according to NYLON Manila.
